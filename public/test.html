PennApps Accelerator

PennApps Accelerator is a community of hackers, entrepreneurs, and builders who want to make the world a better place. As the community for student entrepreneurs at Penn State, we a look to foster great ideas into real startups and projects. For more information on how to get involved, check out www.innoblue.org.

We’d like to welcome & introduce the 2014 Innoblue Accelerator Class!

The following teams will spend the next 8 weeks turning their ideas into reality! The Innoblue Accelerator Team will assign each team a coach who will guide the teams through the accelerator’s weekly workshops and help prepare them for the Startup Showcase. Through the accelerator, the ventures will have access to invaluable resources and pro bono services addressing growth hacking/marketing, financial projection, legal advisory, and many other services within the State College community. At the end of the accelerator, the ventures will have the opportunity to network with industry experts and pitch to potential investors at the Startup Showcase. The Innoblue Team looks forward to accelerating these ventures in the coming weeks so be sure to check the blog each week to see their progress!

Interested in joining, mentoring, or funding the accelerator teams? Contact Mitch@innoblue.org or Dan@innoblue.org .

H20 Pal

h2opal.com

H20Pal is a startup working on innovative methods that help keep you fit and healthy in a nonintrusive and fun way.

Our first focus is on keeping people hydrated. Staying hydrated helps you stay healthy, and information on what you’re putting into your body is important. Many people are chronically dehydrated, and mistreat the symptoms using coffee and medicine when they really need water. This is where we come in.

H2O-Pal monitors your water intake and helps you stay hydrated by giving you reminders when you need to drink water through a supplemental mobile application. It’s a small device you simply attach to the base of a bottle. The device is completely nonintrusive and the product’s design adapts for use with different water bottles.

Improving Fit

groupfitness.improvingfit.com

Improving Fit intends to become the most well rounded brand in health technology. We believe that until now, fitness tracking has been too difficult, and the solutions, too specific. We want to be the company that takes wellness technology to the next level and allow millions of users to work together to create a healthier, happier, world.

Our technologies are created around 3 main goals:

1) To make it easy and fun to track anything that affects a user’s fitness and well-being.

2) To create a platform allowing leaders in health, fitness, nutrition, sports, and disease prevention fields to promote their good work and better the lives of others.

3) To connect users with similar goals and interest, while creating a community that fosters positive change.

Support With Love

Support with Love is the sole non-profit startup in the spring 2014 class. As an idea that sprung for the world’s largest student pitch competition, 1000 Pitches , this venture has come in with a $1000 prize grant as seed funding. Support with Love is a non-profit organization dedicated to creating a community of support and education for Asian Americans dealing with stigmas of mental health; with an emphasis on suicide prevention.

Meet the Team

Lydia Huang — Founder

Lydia lost her best friend to suicide in May 2009, and became inspired to create this organization after reading about mental health stigmas in the Asian American culture. Support with Love will develop and offer culturally-tailored educational workshops for Asian American parents to accept mental health as a valid medical concern, recognize signs of mental health issues, and understand the importance of seeking help and providing support for their children. By way of these educational workshops, Lydia hopes to drastically minimize the stigmas around mental health, and aid in suicide prevention.
